Assistance with SOSSmartphone

Description

SOSsmartphone is the TIM service to all customers, or Rechargeable Subscription, you have to receive specialized, personalized technical assistance for the management of your smartphone or tablet. Thanks to SOSsmartphone service you can talk to an expert who will give you all the advice and "premium" service that you require and that above will be able to remotely connect and operate directly on your device. The services that will be given for SOSsmartphone are: • Data transfer support in case of change phone: example transfer phonebook, photos, text messages; • consultancy in the use of advanced features of the smartphone; • specialist assistance on advanced configurations of the smartphone and integration into your home network; • managing and synchronizing your media; • support to ensure maximum safety; • conservation and protection of your data; • transfer of content; • updating your smartphone; • performing check-ups. To request SOSsmartphone service you can: • contact number 0458214811 (toll call based on your rate / supply plan); • download the App SOS Smartphone APP Store (for iOS) and Google Play (for Android) and request the assistance. The SOSsmartphone service provides a cost to intervention from 3 € (VAT included). The amount will be charged on the invoice if you are a customer with Subscription and will be deducted from the remaining balance if you are a customer Rechargeable. May 12 also the SOSsmartphone service is also available by subscription at 2 € / month. This will give you the assistance you need always available and complete for a year.
